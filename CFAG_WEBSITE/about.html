About Us

Get to know who we are, what we believe, and where we're going.

Our Mission

CFAG Church exists to glorify God by making disciples of Jesus Christ in every generation. We seek to be a Christ-centered, Spirit-empowered, and mission-focused community.

Our Vision

We envision a church where people from every background encounter Jesus, grow in spiritual maturity, and are equipped to serve their families, neighborhood, and the nations.

Our Beliefs

  • The Bible is God's inspired Word and our final authority.
  • There is one God eternally existent in three Persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.
  • Salvation is by grace through faith in Jesus Christ alone.
  • The Church is the body of Christ, called to make disciples of all nations.
  • We eagerly await the return of Jesus Christ and the restoration of all things.

Our Story

CFAG Church began with a small group of believers gathering in a living room to pray for their city. Over the years, God has grown our community into a multi-generational family following Jesus together.

From the beginning, we have been committed to God's presence, biblical teaching, and sending people into their neighborhoods, workplaces, and the nations with the good news of Jesus.
